Terms and Conditions

Thank you for participating in our questionnaire for the voice synthesis model aimed at capturing pronunciation of Trinidadian and Tobagonian English Creole words. Before proceeding, please carefully read and agree to the following terms and conditions regarding data collection:

  1. Data Collection and Usage:
    By agreeing to this form, you consent to the collection of data about you as you interact with all components of the web application. This includes but is not limited to your interactions with the questionnaire and any other features present on the website.

  2. Demographic Data:
    We require demographic data about you to better understand your knowledge of Trinidadian accents. While providing this information is optional, it greatly aids us in improving our application and enhancing user experience.

  3. Rationale for Data Collection:
    • Place of Residence:
      Your place of residence helps us determine the uniqueness and validity of pronunciation based on your background. This allows us to tailor the voice synthesis model to better suit the diversity of Trinidadian and Tobagonian English Creole speakers.

    • Level of Education:
      Understanding your level of education assists us in gauging the range of knowledge you may have regarding the existence and usage of certain words. This insight aids in refining the accuracy and effectiveness of the voice synthesis model.

    • Age Range:
      Collecting data on your age range helps us understand the breadth of your knowledge and the likelihood of exposure to certain words in Trinidadian and Tobagonian English Creole. This information enables us to cater to a wide range of user demographics effectively.

  4. Confidentiality:
    We are committed to protecting your privacy and confidentiality. All data collected will be used solely for research and development purposes related to improving our voice synthesis model. Your personal information will not be shared with any third parties without your explicit consent.

  5. Voluntary Participation:
    Participation in this questionnaire is entirely voluntary. You may choose not to provide certain demographic information or discontinue participation at any time without penalty.
